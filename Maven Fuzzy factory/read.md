# 📊 Maven Fuzzy Factory Dashboard (Power BI)


# 🗂 Dataset

The dataset simulates a real ecommerce business and contains the following tables:

### Fact Tables
- Orders – transaction level order data  
- Order Items – individual product purchases  
- Website Sessions – user sessions and traffic sources  
- Website Pageviews – user navigation behavior  
- Order Item Refunds – refunded items  

### Dimension Tables
- Products – product catalog  
- Calendar – date dimension  

---

# 📈 Dashboard Pages

The dashboard consists of **four analytical pages**.

---

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level business snapshot.

**Key KPIs**
- Total Revenue
- Revenue Last Year
- Year-over-Year Growth
- Previous Month Revenue
- Month-over-Month Growth

**Visuals**
- Revenue trend vs last year
- Revenue by product
- Sales conversion funnel
- Revenue by device

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Performance

Focuses on **transaction and product performance**.

**Key KPIs**
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Refund Rate

**Analysis**
- Revenue by product
- Gross profit by product
- Refund rate by product
- Revenue by campaign
- Monthly revenue trend

---

## 3️⃣ Marketing Performance

Analyzes **marketing channel effectiveness**.

**Key KPIs**
- Total Sessions
- Conversion Rate
- Revenue per Session
- Repeat Session %

**Visuals**
- Sessions by traffic source
- Conversion rate by campaign
- Revenue by traffic source
- Revenue vs sessions comparison
- Conversion trend over time

---

## 4️⃣ Conversion Analysis

Provides a deep dive into the **customer purchase journey**.

**Key KPIs**
- Total Sessions
- Product View Rate
- Cart Conversion Rate
- Checkout Conversion Rate

**Visuals**
- Customer purchase funnel
- Conversion rate trend
- Conversion by device
- Cart conversion by device
- Conversion rate by traffic source
